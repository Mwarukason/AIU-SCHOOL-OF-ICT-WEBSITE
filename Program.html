UNDERGRADUATE DEGREE PROGRAMs

Programes Offered:

All of ICT’s Academic Programmes are approved by Ministry of Higher Education before it is being conducted and offered for registraition to students. This is a requirement under the Private Higher Educational Institutions (Conducting Course of Study) Regulations 1997. All of AIU’s programmes have been granted at least Malaysian Quality Agency (MQA) Provisional Accreditation when it is conducted. Having Provisional Accreditation means that the programme has met the minimum MQA requirements to start conducting the programme.
